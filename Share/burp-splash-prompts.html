Burp Suite Custom Splash Screen

Replace Burp Suite's splash screen with a custom AI-generated design. This was tested on Windows, results may vary on macOS/Linux.

How It Works

The splash PNG is embedded inside BurpSuite.exe. The offset varies between Burp versions but can be auto-detected by scanning for the PNG header. Resize to 912x510, compress under 183KB, patch the bytes, restart Burp.

Image Requirements

912x510 pixels, RGBA PNG, under 186,864 bytes (~183KB) compressed. Dark navy (#111a2e) base with orange (#FF6633) accents.

The splash PNG can be located by searching for the PNG header bytes 89 50 4E 47 0D 0A 1A 0A inside the binary. Look for the 912x510 PNG (the 1x version). On the version tested, it was found at offset 0x770E8. In HxD: Ctrl+G, type 770E8, and you'll land right on it.

The Watchtower

A lone figure on a server tower overlooking a dark cyber city.

Create an illustration for a software splash screen.

Canvas: 1536x1024 pixels. PNG with real alpha transparency.

SCENE: A lone hooded figure in a dark cloak standing on top of a server rack tower, overlooking a vast dark cityscape made of network infrastructure and buildings. The figure holds a glowing orange lantern with a lightning bolt symbol inside it, arm extended outward. The lantern light reveals the city below. The city streets are glowing orange data streams and network connection lines flowing between dark buildings. Orange glowing lights dot the buildings like windows. Dark spires and antenna towers in the background. Network cables hanging from the server rack the figure stands on.

STYLE:
- Dark navy (#111a2e) as the dominant base color
- Orange/amber (#ff6a00) as the ONLY accent color
- No other colors - everything is dark navy, black, or orange
- Professional digital illustration
- Atmospheric, moody, cinematic
- The character is positioned center-right within the shape

SHAPE AND TRANSPARENCY:
- LEFT edge: straight, clean, solid dark navy
- BOTTOM edge: straight, clean, solid dark navy
- RIGHT side: rough, torn, painterly brush strokes fading into full transparency
- TOP side: rough, jagged paint splatters fading into full transparency
- About 20-25% of canvas is fully transparent (alpha=0)

TEXT AND LOGO (blend naturally into the scene):
- The bottom-left area of the scene should naturally be dark and empty (deep shadow, no buildings or detail)
- Place an orange lightning bolt icon and "Burp Suite" in white bold text in this dark area
- The text should feel like part of the artwork, not a separate box or overlay
- NO version number, NO other text anywhere
